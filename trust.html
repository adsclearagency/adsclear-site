Built on Trust & Transparency

AdsClear Agency believes trust is earned through clarity, control, and honest communication. Here is exactly how we protect your interests and keep your campaigns transparent from start to finish.

🔒

You Always Control Your Ad Spend

Your advertising budget goes directly to TikTok through your own Ads Manager account. AdsClear Agency does not hold your ad budget and does not access your payment details. We only manage campaigns after you grant official partner access in TikTok Business Center.

Standard setup • You can remove access at any time.
💰

Transparent Pricing

Our management fees are listed clearly so you know exactly what you are paying for. Your management fee is always separate from TikTok ad spend.

See the full pricing breakdown on our FAQ page.
📊

Estimates Based on TikTok Data

Any estimated click ranges or budget guidance shown on our site are based on forecasting data provided directly inside TikTok Ads Manager. We do not invent numbers or promise guaranteed outcomes.

Results always depend on creative, offer, targeting, and market conditions.
🛡️

Client-First Safeguards

We use official partner access instead of asking for your TikTok login. That means you keep ownership of your account, your billing, and your assets while we manage the campaign.

Your business stays in control throughout the process.
📍

Clear Targeting Boundaries

We can target campaigns by country, region, city, and audience type. This helps reduce overlap and keeps campaigns focused on the market you actually want to reach.

Useful for both LIVE agencies and local or online businesses.
⭐

Growing With Real Proof

As AdsClear Agency grows, this is where client feedback, case studies, and campaign results will be shared. We would rather earn credibility through real outcomes than fill the site with exaggerated claims.

Real proof will be added as campaigns are completed.

Additional Trust Builders

  • Your ad spend is always paid directly to TikTok through your own Ads Manager account.
  • Our management fee is separate and clearly explained before any campaign begins.
  • We use official Business Center partner access instead of asking for your TikTok password.
