FAQ

Do I pay AdsClear Agency for the ads too?

No. Your ad spend is paid directly to TikTok through your own TikTok Ads Manager account. AdsClear Agency charges a separate management fee for setting up, managing, and optimizing the campaigns.

How do payments work?

You add your own payment method inside TikTok Ads Manager under Billing. TikTok charges you for the ad spend. Our management fee is paid separately to AdsClear Agency.

How long does each plan last?

The Starter Campaign lasts for 1 month and is designed as a test phase. Monthly Management and Growth Management are ongoing monthly services and continue as long as you want us managing the campaigns.

Do I need a TikTok account to run ads?

Not always. Some campaigns can run as ads without posting publicly on your profile. If you want the promoted video to appear directly on your page, then a TikTok account is recommended.

Can the ad appear on our TikTok page?

Yes. We can either promote a video from your TikTok profile or run it as a separate ad. We can also direct clicks to your signup page, website, or TikTok profile depending on your preference.

Who creates the video?

In most cases, the client records the recruitment or promotional video since it often performs better when it feels natural and authentic. We can guide the script, messaging, and structure.

Can you target a specific country, region, or city?

Yes. Campaigns can be targeted by country, state, region, city, age range, and other audience filters depending on the campaign goal.

If you work with multiple agencies, will they compete with each other?

No. Campaigns can be separated by region, city, or audience so each agency focuses on its own target market.

What results can I expect?

Results depend on the offer, video quality, targeting, and budget. Our goal is to help generate qualified applications, leads, or customer traffic through properly targeted TikTok campaigns.

What do you need from me to get started?

We typically need your preferred ad budget, your preferred target region, your signup page or website link, your TikTok account username if applicable, and partner access to your TikTok Business Center or Ads Manager account.

How do I add AdsClear Agency to my TikTok account?

You add AdsClear Agency as a partner inside TikTok Business Center using our Business Center ID, then grant access to your ad account so we can manage your campaigns.
